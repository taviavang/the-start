    • In this Museum series, Smiski imitates famous works of art. There are 6 to collect in this series, plus a secret one to find:

    • Smiski The Source - of water, a spring of poetic inspiration.
    • Smiski Fujin and Smiski Raijin - feared and respected Gods of weather.
    • Smiski Bacchus - Smiski is inviting you to join him in a drink of wine.
    • Smiski Little is too young to drink, so he eats the Grapes.
    • Smiski Dali - Smiski is the “camembert of time” in this surreal pose.
    • Smiski Pearl Earring - Smiski is as mysterious as the Girl. Is he looking at you?
    • Smiski Vela’zquez - Smiski, a Knight of Santlago, paints his self-portrait.

    Art isn't just about galleries; it's a treasure trove of stories, especially when our Smiski figures are involved! Each one from the Museum Series brings with it a quirky twist on a classic masterpiece, turning art into an adventure of delightful discoveries. (Fun fact: I have the Smiski Velazquez, it is such a cute figure and I love it! The next one that I want is the smiski pearl earring.)
